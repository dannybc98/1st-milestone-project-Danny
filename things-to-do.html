Visit Madeira Things To do Travel Info

Porto Santo

Porto Santo is a serene and picturesque island in the Madeira archipelago, known for its golden sandy beaches and warm climate. It offers a tranquil escape for beach lovers and history enthusiasts alike.

Whale & Dolphin watching

H2O Madeira offers captivating and responsible whale and dolphin watching tours, thrilling snorkeling adventures, coastal excursions, stand-up paddleboarding, private charters, and active marine conservation initiatives. Their knowledgeable guides provide engaging experiences, leaving visitors with cherished memories and a deeper appreciation for Madeira's marine wonders.

Pico Ruivo

Visiting Pico Ruivo on Madeira Island offers hikers the chance to explore the island's highest peak and witness breathtaking panoramic views of lush valleys and the Atlantic Ocean. The rugged trails and stunning landscapes create an unforgettable experience, making it a must-visit destination for nature enthusiasts seeking tranquility and beauty.
